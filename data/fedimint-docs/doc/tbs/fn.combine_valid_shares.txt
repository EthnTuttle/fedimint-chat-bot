Expand description

Combines a sufficient amount of valid blinded signature shares to a blinded signature. The responsibility of verifying the
supplied shares lies with the caller.

 * sig_shares: an iterator yielding pairs of key indices and signature shares from said key
 * threshold: number of shares needed to combine a signature
